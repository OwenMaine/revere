About Us

About Revere Restaurant Equipment and Supply Co., Inc.

Revere Restaurant Equipment and Supply Co., Inc., located at 177 Winnisimmet St in Chelsea, MA, has been a cornerstone of the local foodservice industry for over two decades. We're more than just a supplier; we're a partner committed to helping restaurants, caterers, bakeries, and other culinary businesses thrive. From equipping a brand-new kitchen to providing essential day-to-day supplies, we offer a comprehensive range of products and services designed to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Our extensive inventory includes everything from heavy-duty cooking equipment like commercial ovens, ranges, and fryers to refrigeration solutions, food preparation tools, and stylish dining room furniture. We carefully curate our selection, partnering with leading manufacturers known for their quality, durability, and innovation. This ensures that our customers receive reliable equipment that can withstand the rigors of a demanding commercial kitchen environment.

Beyond equipment, we understand the importance of having the right supplies on hand to keep your operation running smoothly. That's why we stock a vast array of kitchen supplies, including cookware, bakeware, utensils, storage containers, and cleaning products. We also offer a wide selection of tableware, glassware, and serving pieces to enhance your customers' dining experience.

What truly sets us apart is our unwavering commitment to customer service. Our knowledgeable and experienced team is dedicated to providing personalized support and guidance every step of the way. Whether you need help selecting the right equipment for your specific needs, troubleshooting a technical issue, or simply placing an order, we're here to assist you. We pride ourselves on building long-term relationships with our customers, becoming a trusted partner they can rely on for all their restaurant equipment and supply needs.

We invite you to visit our showroom at 177 Winnisimmet St in Chelsea, MA, or browse our online catalog to discover the wide range of products and services we offer. Let Revere Restaurant Equipment and Supply Co., Inc. be your partner in success!
